Questions, answered

MBBS abroad & FMGE — your questions answered

Straight answers to what students and parents ask us most. Still unsure? Our counsellors guide you free of charge.

Is an MBBS from the Philippines or Timor-Leste valid in India?

Yes. LIMRA's partner universities are aligned with India's National Medical Commission (NMC) norms and award internationally recognised degrees. To practise medicine in India, every foreign medical graduate must clear the Foreign Medical Graduate Examination (FMGE), and LIMRA's No.1 FMGE coaching prepares you for exactly that.

Do I need to qualify NEET to study MBBS abroad?

Yes. Indian students must qualify NEET to pursue an MBBS abroad and to be eligible for medical licensure in India. LIMRA guides you through eligibility, documentation, and admissions from start to finish.

What is the FMGE, and what is LIMRA's pass rate?

The Foreign Medical Graduate Examination (FMGE) is the licensing exam Indian students must clear to practise after an overseas MBBS. LIMRA's FMGE coaching has a 92% pass rate over the past decade — consistently above the national average.

Why study MBBS in the Philippines or Timor-Leste?

NMC-aligned curricula, English-medium instruction, internationally recognised degrees, strong clinical exposure, and significantly lower cost than private medical seats in India. LIMRA partners with six universities across the two countries.

How much does an MBBS abroad cost through LIMRA?

Tuition varies by university and is among the most affordable routes to a medical degree. LIMRA's counsellors share current, detailed fee structures for each partner college — completely free, with no obligation.

What's included in LIMRA's FMGE coaching?

800 hours of training across 19 subjects, 50 mock tests with detailed discussions, a one-year FMGE app subscription, and FMG Exam training worth ₹1,00,000 — all included for enrolled students.

What support does LIMRA provide, end to end?

Everything from your first enquiry to your medical career: free counselling and college selection, admissions and student visa, travel and accommodation, on-ground support during studies, and No.1 FMGE coaching on your return.

Which universities does LIMRA partner with?

Six — Lyceum Northwestern University, Davao Medical School Foundation, Brokenshire College, and Gullas College of Medicine in the Philippines; Universidade Católica Timorense and University of PEACE in Timor-Leste.

How experienced is LIMRA, and how do I start?

LIMRA has guided students for 24+ years, and 2000+ have become doctors with our support. To begin, book free counselling — call +91-94443 75000 or submit the enquiry form, and our advisors will call you back.
