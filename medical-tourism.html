What is Medical Tourism?

Healthcare beyond borders

Travelling abroad for medical, dental or surgical care — at world-class quality, for 60–80% less than Western nations charge.

Asia has become the global leader in medical tourism, and the Philippines stands out: JCI-accredited hospitals, internationally trained specialists, and a fully English-speaking environment that removes the language barrier other destinations can't.

LIMRA leverages 24 years of relationships across the Philippines and Southeast Asia to coordinate your entire journey — so you receive premium care without navigating a foreign healthcare system alone.

Our Services

Complete medical tourism support

From your first enquiry to your return home — LIMRA is with you every step of the way.

Hospital Selection

We match your medical needs with the right hospital and specialist from our network of JCI-accredited hospitals in the Philippines.

Medical Consultation

Pre-travel consultation with specialists to review your case, plan treatment, and prepare you — before you even board the flight.

Travel Coordination

Flight booking, visa assistance and airport transfers — all arranged, with attention to your medical needs throughout the journey.

Accommodation

Comfortable stays near the hospital, suited to recovery — from budget options to premium facilities, based on your preferences.

On-Ground Support

A local coordinator is with you throughout — hospital accompaniment, translation if needed, and help with anything that arises.

Post-Treatment Follow-up

We coordinate follow-up consultations and your transition back to your home doctor — ensuring continuity of care after you return.
