What is Medical Tourism?

Healthcare Beyond Borders

Medical tourism involves traveling to another country to receive medical, dental, or surgical care. Asia has emerged as the global leader in medical tourism, offering world-class healthcare at 60–80% lower costs than Western nations.

LIMRA leverages its deep connections in Philippines and Southeast Asia to coordinate your entire medical journey — ensuring you receive premium care without the complexities and costs of navigating a foreign healthcare system alone.

Whether you need specialized surgery, complex diagnostics, dental care, or wellness treatments, LIMRA connects you with the right hospital and specialist while managing all logistics.

Our Services

Complete Medical Tourism Support

From your first enquiry to your return home — LIMRA is with you every step of the way.

🏥

Hospital Selection

We match your medical needs with the right hospital and specialist from our network of JCI-accredited hospitals in Philippines — ensuring the best care for your specific condition.

Medical Consultation

Pre-travel consultation with specialists to review your case, provide treatment plans, and prepare you for the procedure — before you even board the flight.

Travel Coordination
