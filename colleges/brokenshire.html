Recognition & partnerships

Accreditations & Collaborations

Brokenshire College of Medicine is backed by institutional accreditations that validate the quality of its education — and the eligibility of its graduates to practise internationally.

PAASCU Accredited

Brokenshire College is accredited by the Philippine Accrediting Association of Schools, Colleges and Universities (PAASCU), affirming a standard of academic excellence sustained over decades of medical education.

NMC Recognised

Brokenshire is listed in the World Directory of Medical Schools and recognised by India’s National Medical Commission — LIMRA graduates are eligible to sit the FMGE on return to India.

WHO-AVICENNA Listed

The institution is included in the WHO’s AVICENNA directory of medical schools, providing global recognition for graduates seeking licensure or postgraduate training in multiple countries.
