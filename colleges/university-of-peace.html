Why Choose University of PEACE

Quality Medicine in Southeast Asia

University of PEACE (UNPAZ) in Dili, Timor-Leste offers an internationally oriented medical education with significantly lower costs than other destinations — and LIMRA's full backing to ensure every student succeeds.

Genuinely Affordable Education

Lower tuition and living costs compared to the Philippines — making quality medical education accessible for more Indian families.

Internationally Oriented MD Program

A medical degree aligned to international standards — preparing students to clear FMGE and practice medicine in India and globally.

Practical Clinical Training

Hospital rotations in Dili's major healthcare facilities — hands-on experience that builds clinical thinking from the early years of study.

Multicultural Learning Environment

Study alongside students from across Asia in a campus that celebrates diversity and builds cross-cultural medical competence.

Full LIMRA Support — Start to Finish

Admission, visa, travel, settlement, and FMGE prep — LIMRA's 24-year track record means no student navigates this journey alone.
