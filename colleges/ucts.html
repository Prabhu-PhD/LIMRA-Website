Why Choose UCTS

A Different Path to the Same Goal

UCTS offers a rare opportunity — internationally recognized medical education in Timor-Leste, Southeast Asia's newest nation. Smaller batches mean more individual attention, and LIMRA's full support means you are never alone.

Small Class Sizes — More Attention

Smaller student batches mean personalized academic support, closer faculty relationships, and a learning environment where no one falls behind.

Truly Affordable Medical Education

Significantly lower tuition and living costs compared to Philippines colleges — making MBBS accessible without compromising on quality.

Unique International Learning Experience

Study in Southeast Asia's youngest nation — a multicultural environment that builds global perspective and cross-cultural medical competence.

Clinical Training in Real Hospitals

Hospital rotations at Timor-Leste's national hospital and affiliated regional centres — practical, patient-centred clinical exposure from early years.

Full LIMRA Support Throughout

From admission and visa through graduation and FMGE prep — LIMRA's 24-year experience means you have expert guidance at every stage.

Recognition & partnerships

Accreditations & Collaborations

UCT’s medical programme holds recognition from Timor-Leste’s health authorities and international bodies — ensuring its graduates are eligible for licensing in India and other jurisdictions.

Ministry of Health Recognised

Universidade Católica Timorense is recognised by the Ministry of Health of Timor-Leste, certifying that its medical programme meets the nation’s public health and professional standards.

NMC Recognised

UCT is listed in the World Directory of Medical Schools and recognised by India’s National Medical Commission — enabling its graduates to sit the FMGE and practise medicine in India after clearing the screening examination.

International Collaboration

UCT maintains academic collaborations with international medical institutions, broadening research and clinical training opportunities for its students and faculty.
