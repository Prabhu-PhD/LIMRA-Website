Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to LIMRA Overseas Education ("we", "our", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP Act, 2023).

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when expressing an interest in obtaining information about us or our services. This includes:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address.
  • Educational Interests: Selected courses, preferred university destinations.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal information to:

  • Contact you regarding your enquiry (via phone call, WhatsApp, or email).
  • Provide personalized guidance and counselling for overseas medical education.
  • Send you updates about admissions, deadlines, and coaching schedules.

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Processors

We do not sell your personal data. We share your data only with trusted third-party service providers necessary to operate our website and services:

  • Web3Forms: We use Web3Forms to securely process our website contact forms. Data submitted via our forms is routed through their servers.
  • Analytics Providers: We use aggregated, anonymized data to analyze website traffic.

4. Your Rights

Under the DPDP Act, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time.
  • Request the erasure of your personal data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
