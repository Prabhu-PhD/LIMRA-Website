Our Story

Built on trust, proven by results

LIMRA was founded on one conviction — every deserving student in India should have a real, honest path to becoming a doctor.

Two decades ago, families chasing an MBBS abroad faced a maze of agents, hidden donations, and broken promises. We set out to be the opposite: transparent fees, recognised universities, and genuine guidance from enquiry to graduation.

Today we partner with six hand-picked universities across the Philippines and Timor-Leste — each chosen for NMC alignment, clinical quality, and proven FMGE outcomes. And our free in-house coaching has grown into South India's No.1 FMGE program.

Our Journey

Two decades, one mission

The milestones that turned a single Chennai office into South India's most trusted name in overseas medical education.

2001

LIMRA opens its doors

Founded in Chennai with a single promise — an honest, affordable path to an MBBS abroad, with no hidden donations.

2008

The first 100 doctors

Our early batches clear the screening exam and begin practising across India — proof that the model works.

2014

Free FMGE coaching launches

In-house coaching across all 19 subjects — included free for every student, and soon South India's No.1 program.

2019

Partnerships expand abroad

Direct tie-ups across the Philippines and Timor-Leste — every university NMC-aligned and WHO-recognised.

2026

2,000+ doctors and counting

Twenty-four years on, LIMRA stands as South India's most trusted overseas medical education consultancy.
