Our Story

More Than a Consultancy — A Lifelong Partner

LIMRA was founded with a single mission: to make quality medical education accessible to every deserving student, regardless of financial barriers. For over 24 years, we have been the bridge between ambitious Indian students and world-class medical universities in Philippines and Timor-Leste.

What sets LIMRA apart is our end-to-end commitment. We don't just secure admissions — we guide students through their entire medical journey, from college selection and visa processing to FMGE coaching and career counselling after graduation.

Authorized Representative for premier medical universities in Philippines and Timor-Leste — ensuring authentic admissions with full institutional support.

No.1 FMGE Coaching Center in South India — 92% pass rate over the past decade, consistently above the national average.

Transparent and Honest Guidance — No hidden fees, no false promises. Only the right college for each student's profile and budget.

Continuous Student Support — Our relationship with students doesn't end at admission. We support them throughout their education and beyond.

Our Mission & Vision

What Drives Us

🎯

Our Mission

To make quality medical education abroad accessible to every deserving Indian student by providing honest guidance, authentic university partnerships, and unwavering post-admission support — ensuring every student we enroll becomes a successful doctor.

🌟

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in medical education abroad for Indian students — recognized not just for our 24-year legacy, but for the individual success story of every doctor we have helped create. Quality over quantity. Integrity over profit.

🤝

Our Values

Transparency in every interaction. Honesty about college strengths and limitations. A genuine commitment to student welfare above all else. No college is right for every student — we help you find the right fit, not the most profitable one for us.

🏆

Our Commitment

24 years is not just a number — it's a testament to the trust thousands of families have placed in LIMRA. Every decision we make is guided by the question: "Is this the best outcome for our student?" That commitment will never change.
