Hear It From a Real Student

Have questions that only someone who has lived it can answer? Our student ambassadors are current MBBS students at LIMRA's partner universities — ready to speak with you honestly about life, academics, and making the leap.

How it works

Three simple steps

Pick a university

Browse the ambassadors below and choose someone studying at the university you're most interested in.

Message on WhatsApp

Hit the WhatsApp button on their card. They'll reply and arrange a convenient time for a call or chat.

Ask anything

Campus life, hostel, clinical postings, FMGE prep, costs — no question is off limits. Honest answers only.
