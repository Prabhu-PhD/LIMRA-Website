About the Exam

What is the FMG Exam?

The FMGE is the mandatory screening test every Indian student who studies MBBS abroad must clear to practise medicine in India.

Conducted by the National Board of Examinations (NBE), it tests 300 questions across all 19 medical subjects. A score of 150+ is required to pass — but with the national pass rate often below 20%, the right coaching is what makes the difference.

LIMRA's students consistently outperform the national average, clearing the exam at a 92% rate over the last decade.

January 2026: 21 LIMRA students scored above 200 marks — a standout result that reflects the depth of our program.
