About FMGE

What is the FMG Exam?

The Foreign Medical Graduate Examination (FMGE / NEXT) is a mandatory screening test conducted by the National Board of Examinations (NBE) for Indian citizens who have obtained primary medical qualifications (MBBS or equivalent) from foreign medical institutions.

Passing FMGE is required for any foreign medical graduate to practice medicine in India. The exam tests knowledge across 19 medical subjects with a total of 300 questions. A score of 150+ is required to pass.

With the national FMGE pass rate often below 20%, the right coaching makes all the difference. LIMRA's students consistently outperform the national average with a 92% pass rate.

Key Fact: In the January 2026 FMG Exam, 21 LIMRA students scored above 200 marks — demonstrating the quality of our coaching program.
